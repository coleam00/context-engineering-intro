REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
System: POS and Back Office
Industry: Retail Sport Fashion
 
1. Company Information
Company Description
OTCF S.A. is a leading Polish company operating in the clothing and sports industry, specializing in the design, production, and distribution of high-quality sportswear and accessories under brands such as 4F, 4FJunior, 4F Fuel, and Under Armour (Poland distributor). The company operates an extensive network of brick-and-mortar stores and online stores (4F and Sport Style Story) and sells through wholesale channels.
 
Mission and Values
OTCF S.A. aims to promote an active lifestyle by delivering high-quality sportswear and accessories. The company places strong emphasis on innovation, product quality, and sustainable development, and supports athletes and sporting events to promote a healthy and active lifestyle.
 
Brands and Products
•	4F – The flagship brand offering sportswear and accessories, known for high-quality materials, modern design, and functionality.
•	4F Junior – Sportswear and accessories for children aged 6–12, encouraging them to explore various sports and activities.
•	4F Fuel – A brand aimed at physically active people at all levels, offering functional food and dietary supplements.
•	Under Armour – Since 2020, OTCF S.A. has been the exclusive distributor of Under Armour in Poland, with wholesale distribution rights and a license to open monobrand stores.
 
Store Network
•	Store Brands (signage): 4F, Sport Style Story, Multibrand
•	Store Types:
 • Company-owned regular stores
 • Company-owned outlets
 • Franchise regular stores
 • Shop-in-shop counters
 • Seasonal stands
 
Markets and Points of Sale:
•	Poland – 450 POS (avg. 2 cash registers + 1 back-office PC per store)
•	Slovakia – 22
•	Romania – 21
•	Lithuania – 8
•	Latvia – 6
•	Croatia – 3
•	Czech Republic – 2
•	Planned: Bulgaria, Hungary, Estonia, Montenegro, Ukraine, and others
 
POS Environment:
All locations use a POS system integrated with ERP (D365) and peripherals such as:
fiscal printer, payment terminal, customer entry counter, standard printer, label printer, waybill printer, barcode scanner, cash drawer, customer display.
 
2. Project Objectives
Replace the current POS and Back Office system with a modern, lightweight technology solution that:
•	Simplifies installation and maintenance
•	Enables easy integration with other systems
•	Increases flexibility and reduces time for changes
•	Improves data synchronization
 
The solution should support not only checkout operations but also warehouse, accounting, reporting, and integration with internal and external systems. The desktop version should be complemented by a mobile app for handheld scanner devices (CipherLab, Bluebird).
 
3. Scope of the RFP
3.1 Functional Requirements
Product Catalogue / Stock / Prices
•	Display product data (basic info + attributes + images + descriptions), prices (list, current, “omnibus”), stock levels (sales floor / back office)
•	Show complementary, additional, and substitute products
 
Warehouse Management
•	Handle stock movements (deliveries, store-to-store transfers, RW/PW docs) and discrepancies
•	Manage transfer and return orders to central warehouse
•	Register courier shipments with status tracking
 
Pricing / Discounts / Promotions
•	Update prices by price groups
•	Announce upcoming price changes
•	Print labels with list/current/omnibus prices
•	Define promotions for network/store groups/single stores
•	Configure promotion rules, exclusions, dependencies, stacking order, minimum price thresholds, etc.
•	Create and process coupons, loyalty cards (with limits), vouchers
 
Sales & Returns
•	Issue sales (receipt, invoice) and returns (receipt correction, invoice correction)
•	Support multiple payment types (cash, card, gift card, voucher, mixed)
•	Integrate with payment terminals
•	Post-fiscalization of receipts
•	Comply with KSEF and equivalents abroad
 
Gift Cards
•	Generate/import cards from external systems
•	Load/reload cards, process refunds to gift cards
 
Cash Operations
•	Record movements between tills, safe, and bank
•	End-of-shift settlement
•	Cash reports
 
Inventory
•	Full and partial inventory
•	Import results files
 
Complaints
•	Register complaints, process with central system integration (decisions, statuses)
•	Refund/settlement with customer
 
Damaged Goods
•	Register damaged items, decide on disposal, markdown, or return with central system integration
 
Omnichannel & Loyalty
•	In-store pickup and returns of e-commerce orders
•	Handle e-commerce complaints and reservations
•	Check e-commerce stock availability
•	Order products online for delivery or store pickup
•	Ship from store directly to customer
•	Identify omnichannel customers, check their profile
•	Generate customer recommendations based on omnichannel data
 
Store Staff Management
•	Add users, assign roles
•	Track worked hours
•	Assign sales to staff, generate individual sales reports
 
Reporting
•	Import sales/KPI plans (daily, monthly), show achievement status
•	Generate reports and summaries
 
User Interface
•	Modern, simple, responsive, customizable views
•	Multi-language support
 
Intercompany
•	Different functionalities per market according to legal/accounting regulations and business strategy
 
Roles & Access
•	Define users → roles → permissions
•	Assign users to store/region
 
Mobile POS (iOS, Android) – Full product info, recommendations, stock levels (own & other stores), pricing, order handling, deliveries, transfers, returns, inventory, stock replenishment, online ordering, bilingual UI (PL/EN).
 
 
3.2 Technical Requirements
Installation & Deployment (8)
•	MSI installer with silent install
•	POS configuration during imaging/registration
•	Move POS between stores without re-imaging
•	No admin rights required for POS/services
•	DEV, PREPROD, UAT environments hosted by vendor
•	Remote reinstallation option
•	Hardware replacement with same hostname without losing fiscal document sequence
 
Architecture & Security (15)
•	Secure encrypted comms with central system
•	Signed installer and executables
•	OS lock/unlock with PIN per user
•	Store-level accounts only, separate from AD for store staff
•	AD group integration for HQ users
•	Centralized auth logging (user, POS, role, actions)
•	Centralized error logging and monitoring with alerts
•	Audit logs, GDPR compliance, data encryption
 
Hardware & Compatibility (3)
•	Min: Ryzen 5, 16GB RAM, 256GB SSD
•	USB integration with eService terminals
•	Support for Windows 10 & 11
 
System Functionality (10) – Managed updates, offline mode, auto printer config, hotfix without downtime, HA server-side, POS health check module, progress bars, touch screen support, per-process printer config.
 
User Management & Permissions (4) – Central admin control, market-specific roles, unique login generator, NFC card login.
UX/UI & Support (5) – In-app knowledge base, no paid DB license on POS, Layout Manager, efficient on 10/5 Mbps link.
Integrations (8) – Public REST/GraphQL API, external comms via API or queues (RabbitMQ, Apache), add-on system, custom reporting tools, Power BI integration, ad-hoc reporting, MS D365 integration (on-prem & cloud), documented external integrations (loyalty, CRM, CDP, Magento, PIM, cloud analytics, stock sync).
 
3.3 Technical Requirements – Mobile App
•	Platforms: Android 10+, iOS (latest supported)
•	Distribution: MDM Intune (Google Play & Apple Business Manager)
•	Min Hardware: 3GB RAM
•	Hardware Integration: Barcode scanners (Bluebird, CipherLab, Zebra), mobile printers, payment terminals (BT/Wi-Fi/USB)
•	Security: Data encryption, HTTPS, remote config via Intune, compliance with Google Play & Apple guidelines
